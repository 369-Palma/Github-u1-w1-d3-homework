Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Craberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your favourite holiday starter!

Image:Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:

Cranberry Recepies That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¾ cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • ½ teaspoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 ½cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough
